John Dsouza

Operations Manager

As the Operations Manager at Resource Hub, he brings strong operational leadership and valuable experience within a paediatric rehabilitation setting. His background in supporting therapy-based services has equipped him with a clear understanding of the operational and organisational frameworks that enable effective occupational therapy delivery. He plays a key role in streamlining processes, coordinating services, and supporting clinical teams to ensure smooth day-to-day operations. With a strong focus on efficiency, quality, and collaboration, he helps create an environment where therapists can focus on delivering meaningful, client-centred outcomes while supporting the organisation’s growth and service excellence.
