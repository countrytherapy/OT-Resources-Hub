Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

At Occupational Therapy Resource Hub (“we”, “our”, “us”), we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you access our website or services in accordance with UAE data protection laws, including Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name, email, and contact details
  • Business or organisation information
  • Messages or inquiries submitted via contact forms
  • Any other information you provide while interacting with us

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation data
  • Referring websites

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Deliver services and requested information
  • Send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Enhance website performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers assisting with website operations
  • Authorities when required by law
  • Business transfers, such as mergers or acquisitions

Your Rights

Under UAE law, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or update your data
  • Request deletion or removal
  • Withdraw consent for processing
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyse traffic, and improve website functionality. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors without parental consent.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be reflected on this page.
