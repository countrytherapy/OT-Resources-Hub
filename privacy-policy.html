Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

At Occupational Therapy Resource Hub, we are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) as set out in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and manage your personal information when you visit our website or use our digital Occupational Therapy (OT) resources and services.

2. What Personal Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

2.1 Directly from You:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Professional details (e.g., occupational therapist registration number, organisation name)
  • Billing and payment information (via secure third-party providers)

2.2 Automatically via Website Usage:

  • IP address and geolocation
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website links

We only collect information that is necessary for us to deliver our services and operate our business effectively.

3. Why We Collect Your Information

We collect your information to:

  • Provide you access to our OT digital resources
  • Process and manage payments securely
  • Send purchase confirmations, updates, and customer support communications
  • Respond to inquiries or feedback
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Send newsletters or marketing materials (only with your consent)
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, interference, and loss, and from unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. This includes:

  • Secure password-protected systems
  • SSL encryption for transactions
  • Restricted access to authorised personnel only

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

5. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may disclose it to:

  • Third-party service providers (e.g. Stripe, PayPal, Google Analytics, email marketing tools) to help us operate our services
  • Professional advisors (e.g. legal, accounting) where required
  • Government authorities or regulators when required by law

We ensure that any third parties we work with comply with privacy and data protection obligations.

6. Overseas Disclosure

Some of our third-party service providers may store or process personal information outside of Australia (e.g. in the United States or Europe). When this occurs, we ensure those providers meet applicable privacy standards and take reasonable steps to protect your data.

7. Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction if your information is inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated

To do so, please contact us at the details provided below.

8. Your Choices and Marketing Communications

You may opt out of receiving marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly. Even if you opt out, we may still send essential service communications (e.g. invoices, account updates).

9. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Analyse website traffic and usage
  • Enhance site functionality and user experience

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our resources are intended for use by adult professionals and are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal obligations or business practices. The updated policy will be posted on our website with a revised effective date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, complaints, or wish to access or correct your personal information, please contact us at:

Email: reception@countrytherapy.com.au
